The gap

The gap is not awareness. It is workflow.

Most professionals already use AI. They use it for emails, quick searches, the occasional summary. Almost nobody has it integrated into the work that actually fills their week.

The market is saturated at the "I use ChatGPT" level. The next move, integrating AI into specific tasks, automating real workflows, building agents that do recurring work, that move has not happened yet for most people.

Generic AI courses do not close this gap. Vertical, hands-on training does.

FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

What if I am a complete beginner with AI tools?
That is fine. We start by mapping your work, not your tool history. The 3-Hour Intensive and the half-day workshop both work for beginners. We use the scoping call to calibrate the depth.
What if my team already uses ChatGPT every day?
Then you are exactly the audience the survey points to. Past beginner, short of integrated. The 6-Week Coaching Block and the AI Integration Sprint are designed for this case. We push past chat-prompting into automation and agents.
Can you train on tools other than ChatGPT?
Yes. Claude, Gemini, Copilot, plus orchestration tools like n8n, Zapier, and Make. We pick the tool that fits your workflow and your existing stack, not the one with the loudest marketing.
Do you sign NDAs and respect confidentiality?
Yes. We sign NDAs by default for company engagements. For sensitive sectors like healthcare and finance, we discuss data handling and tool choice during the scoping call. No client data leaves the session without explicit permission.
Where do you deliver workshops in person?
Amsterdam by default. On-site across the EU for full-day and multi-week engagements. For most 1:1 work the online format works better and saves you time and money.
Can you invoice my company directly?
Yes. All team workshops and sprints are invoiced. Individual 1:1 sessions can also be invoiced to your employer if your company sponsors learning. We provide a VAT invoice on request.
